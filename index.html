Effects of Plastic

Plastic has become ubiquitous in our daily lives thanks to its convenience and low prices. But it comes with a cost. Our mistreatment and mismanagement of the material have made it one of the biggest environmental problems of our lifetime. Millions of tons of plastic waste are dumped every year, a majority of which makes its way into the oceans, harming wildlife and ecosystems in the process. Yet 91% of all plastic that has ever been made is not recycled.

Our mission

In today's world, responsible plastic use and environmental conservation have become paramount concerns. To address these pressing issues, a dedicated mission is underway, focusing on several key initiatives.

Raising Awareness

This mission involves conducting awareness campaigns, workshops, and public outreach programs to inform people about the importance of responsible plastic use.

Recycling and Waste Management

Promote proper recycling and waste management practices, as well as supporting the development of more efficient recycling technologies. This includes educating people about what can and cannot be recycled and working with municipalities to improve recycling programs.

Educating Youth

Develop and implement educational programs in our school and youth organizations to instill responsible plastic use habits in the next generation. Youth engagement is crucial for long-term change.

Fund Raising

Secure funding through grants, donations, and partnerships to sustain and expand the team's activities and projects.

Giving waste a new form

Plastic is not just so useless, if we can make useful products out of it, that's what are teams main purpose would include.

Advocate for Policy Changes in long run

Work to influence local, regional, and national governments to enact and enforce policies that reduce plastic production and encourage responsible use. This could include advocating for plastic bans, taxation on single-use plastics, and improved recycling infrastructure.
